Welcome to my GSAP Learning Journey

This webpage is a showcase of my exploration into GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) and its powerful animation tools. I’ve used GSAP to bring life to this page with smooth, responsive, and interactive animations.

Through this project, I’ve learned how to use GSAP’s core functionalities and its ScrollTrigger plugin to create scroll-based animations and transitions. Scroll through to see dynamic effects like fading, sliding, scaling, and more!
